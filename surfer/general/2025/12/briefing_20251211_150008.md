If the program scales as planned, it could set a new benchmark for maritime deterrence across the Euro‑Atlantic. Conversely, a slowdown could leave a security vacuum that might compel Poland to accelerate its own defense initiatives.  

A third, often overlooked, lever is the housing allowance for security personnel. The Polish government has approved a **300 zł** monthly allowance, rising to **1800 zł** in high‑cost areas (link: [Nawet 1800 zł miesięcznie](https://www.bankier.pl/wiadomosc/Nawet-1800-zl-miesiecznie-ABW-i-AW-z-dodatkami-mieszkaniowymi-9051593.html)).
