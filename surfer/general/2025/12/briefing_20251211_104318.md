5. **Implications for stakeholders** – For **business leaders**, the key is to embed price‑hedging into procurement. **Investors** should weigh the risk‑return profile of energy‑transition assets versus traditional utilities. **Innovation managers** must align R&D pipelines with the projected demand for low‑carbon solutions.  
6. **Decision point** – If the 400 000‑ton target is met within the next 12 months, it signals a viable business case for scaling CCS; if not, the risk premium on such projects will rise sharply.
