**Predictable Client Acquisition for Consulting Leaders**  
The core of the model is a three‑step sequence: capture attention, build trust, and present an offer. In the pilot launched on 2025‑12‑30, the firm reported a 30 % increase in qualified leads within the first month, driven by consistent weekly touchpoints.  

If the weekly cadence accelerates to bi‑weekly content (every 3 days), the firm could see a 15 % faster conversion rate, but the risk of content fatigue rises, potentially diluting the perceived value of each touch. Conversely, halving the cadence to monthly content would reduce operational overhead by 50 % but could lead to a 20 % drop in lead quality, as prospects lose momentum. A complete reversal—dropping the structured follow‑up—would likely revert the practice to a feast‑or‑famine cycle, with revenue volatility exceeding 40 % month‑over‑month.
